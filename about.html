About Us

We are a group of passionate students at Concordia University on a mission to inspire and empower aspiring developers to build their future.

Our Story

"StartCodingNow" began as a collaborative project for our IT course at Concordia University. As a group of diverse and ambitious students, we wanted to create something impactful that would help beginners in coding. We believe technology is the cornerstone of innovation, and our goal is to make learning it accessible to everyone.
